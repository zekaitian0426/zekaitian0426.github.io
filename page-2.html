Shot 222-240: Woman's story

One of the most memoreable scenes in this film is the scene where the woman, after being raped by the bandit and being discarded, went to unlease her husband samurai who was tied to a pine tree. The samurai, instead of happily reuniting with his beloved wife, demeaned his wife for her loss of purity.

The dramatic tension of this scene is not only told through stories, but also through cinematography. Here we will provide the shot list of this scene, and briefly analyze the effects of each shots on leveling the dramatic tension up.

Shot Type of Shot Descriptions of Shot
#222 Long Shot the woods. With her back to the camera, the woman runs toward her husband; the bandit pushes her, knocking her down, and goes up to the husband bound by the tree. He takes the husband's sword and starts to leave.
#223 Master Shot MS of Tajomaru as he turns to sneer at the husband. The woman's sobs are heard and Tajomaru begins to laugh and point at the husband, then turns away.
#224 Long Shot as Tajomaru stops to laugh again, jumping up and down; then he runs away from the camera, disappearing into the woods.
#225 Long Shot the woman lies weeping on the ground by her husband.
#226 Long Shot The same, from nearer.
#227 Long Shot The same. Nearer still.
#228 Master Close-up of the woman, sobbing; finally she raises her head to look brokenheartedly at her husband.
#229 Master Close-up of her husband.
#230 Master Close-up Reaction shot of #229.
#231 Long Shot from behind the woman as she rises and rushes toward her husband in the background and throws herself on him.
#232 Close-up OTS over her shoulder. She sobs on his breast, looks up and is chocked by what she sees.
#233 Close-up of the husband from over her shoulder. He looks at her coldly, cynically.
#234 Master Close-up of the woman in the prison courtyard as she continues: the woodcutter and priest visible in the background.
#235 Master Shot in the woods, the woman seen over her husband’s shoulder. She pulls herself away from him, staring at him. As she speaks, she moves from side to side before him, the camera moving with her.
#236 Close-up she covers her face with her hands and starts to sink back to the ground.
#237 Extreme Close-up of the top of her head as she lies shaking and sobbing
#238 Close-up suddenly she looks up, glances around, starts to rise.
#239 Long Shot the pair in the background; in the foreground is the dagger, still sticking point first in the ground. She rises to her feet, comes forward and retrieves it, and rushes back to her husband, starting to cut his bonds.
#240 Close-up the dagger cutting through the rope.
